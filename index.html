Hostel Rules & FAQs

What are the curfew timings of Sky Hostels? +

The gates open at 6:00 AM and curfew is strictly enforced at 10:00 PM. No resident is allowed to enter or leave the premises without written warden permissions after curfew hours.

What is the visitor and guest policy? +

Visitors are allowed inside common lobbies only between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM with prior warden permission. Overnight guests are strictly prohibited inside student rooms.

Are there specific rules regarding dining hall food? +

Food must be consumed inside the dining area. Carrying meals into individual student rooms is not permitted unless under genuine medical sickness conditions approved by the warden.

How are payment dues and fee collections structured? +

Monthly hostel fees must be paid in advance by the 5th of each calendar month. Late payments will incur penalties. Deposits are returned upon complete checkout verification.

What is the cleanliness policy for individual rooms? +

Daily cleaning is handled by our staff. However, students must maintain basic cleanliness inside their rooms and keep their secure personal storage organized.

What are the strict disciplinary boundaries? +

Tobacco, alcohol, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited. Loud music or disturbances after 10 PM will lead to immediate disciplinary inquiry and potential eviction.
